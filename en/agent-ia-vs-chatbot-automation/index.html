AI Agent, chatbot, automation: what is the difference?

These terms are often mixed together. Yet they do not solve the same business need.

The chatbot replies

A chatbot mainly talks: it answers questions, guides a visitor or collects initial information.

Automation triggers

Automation follows a rule: if an event happens, then an action occurs.

The AI Agent prepares work

An AI Agent analyzes context, structures information and produces a deliverable to use or approve.

The right choice depends on the result

If you want a conversation, think chatbot. If you want a trigger, think automation. If you want business work prepared, think AI Agent.
