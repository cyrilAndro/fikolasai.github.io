Business guide

ChatGPT for business: save time without creating chaos.

ChatGPT can help your teams write, summarize, analyze, prepare and structure work. But without a method, everyone experiments alone and the results stay uneven.

The goal is to turn ChatGPT into a clear professional practice: the right use cases, prompts, guardrails and habits.

Positioning

We start from tasks and deliverables, not from technology.

Good business use cases

Communication

Prepare emails, client replies, call scripts, LinkedIn posts or internal messages.

Summary

Summarize meetings, documents, notes, client feedback and scattered information.

Structuring

Turn raw material into an outline, checklist, procedure, report or deliverable.

Limits to set

  • Do not share sensitive information without a clear framework.
  • Do not publish or send without human validation.
  • Do not believe a magic prompt replaces a business method.

The right approach

Start with a few repetitive cases, train teams on real examples, then turn recurring uses into more structured AI Agents if they create value.
