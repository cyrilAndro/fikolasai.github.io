Frequently asked questions

Can AI create a complete course?

It can accelerate preparation, but learning objectives, pedagogy, accuracy and final validation remain the trainer’s responsibility.

Does the training cover administration?

Yes. Use cases can include emails, summaries, planning and document preparation.

How is educational quality protected?

Every workflow starts from explicit objectives and includes fact-checking, review and acceptance criteria.
